The market splits into three flavors: omnichannel "platform" players (Numa, Mia, Podium-adjacent), voice-first receptionists (Stella, Pam), and the newer human-feeling, deeply-integrated generation (Flai). Your wedge: built-from-scratch voice AI that sounds human, deep DMS/CRM/scheduler writeback, and a platform that covers lead → appointment with equal strength across fixed and variable ops.

Sales Playbook

How to position Flai in an auto AI deal

A concise field guide to use before and during calls. Use alongside the comparison matrix and per-competitor tabs.

Opening Positioning (60-second version)

"Flai is the only AI communications platform built from scratch to feel human — across phone, text, email, and recalls, spanning sales and service. We integrate deeply with your DMS, CRM, scheduler, and phone system so every lead gets worked and every appointment lands in your book. Toyota Ventures, First Round, and YC back us; Freeman Lexus ran 1,100 calls through us with zero missed and an 88% booking rate."

Three Questions to Ask Every Prospect

  • "What percentage of inbound calls go to voicemail right now — do you know?"
  • "If I played you a 30-second recording of AI answering your service line, could your customers tell the difference?"
  • "Is your sales team getting the same call-handling quality as your service advisors?"

Qualify the Deal in 4 Minutes

Fixed Ops Signals (service-drive)

  • Complaining about advisor overload, BDC turnover, missed calls
  • Using Xtime, MyKaarma, Tekion Scheduler, Dealer-FX
  • CSI tied to GM comp
  • Has a recall backlog
  • Running on CDK / Reynolds / Tekion DMS

Variable Ops Signals (sales)

  • Lead response SLA is > 5 minutes
  • Hot-lead follow-up stops after 3 attempts
  • BDC does sales prospecting in-house
  • Internet leads outnumber phone-up by > 5:1
  • Considering (or already using) a live-person BDC vendor

The One Demo Move That Closes

The A/B Listen
Record 60 seconds of your best Flai service call. Ask the GM to close their eyes. Play Flai first, then a competitor's best public demo call. Ask: "Which one would you want greeting your customers at 7:47 PM on a Thursday when you're closed?" Nine times out of ten, the answer is Flai — and that's the moment they lean in.
